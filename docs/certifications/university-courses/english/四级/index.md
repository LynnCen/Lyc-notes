练习案例1：Self-discipline

The Importance of Self-discipline

[Introduction] Nowadays, the importance of self-discipline has sparked heated debate among the public. It is generally believed that self-discipline plays a vital role in our daily lives.

[Analysis] To begin with, self-discipline enables us to cultivate healthy habits, which ensures we make continuous progress in our work and academic study. Moreover, it can prevent us from laziness and anxiety, making our lives more fulfilling and meaningful.

[Conclusion] In conclusion, self-discipline is the key to success. Without it, we can accomplish nothing. As college students, we should cultivate this habit consciously. We are advised to set strict plans for both our studies and daily lives, and more importantly, stick to them faithfully.

## 四级作文万能模版整理

### 一、三段通用模版

第一段（引出话题）：

Nowadays, there is a growing discussion about [topic].  
It has sparked heated debate among the public / college students.  
It is generally believed that [topic] plays a vital role in our daily lives / personal growth.  
To elaborate on my point, we should consider it from the following perspectives.

第二段（论证分析）总结构：

To begin with, [argument 1]. is characterized by its wide variety of xxx. So that it is not only help you to accumulate adequate knowledge of xxx around the world, bug also arouse your passion for the xxx and bring you an opportunity to make new friends.

Moreover, [argument 2].  
There is no denying that [topic] enables us to [具体能力/结果], which is beneficial to our future study / career / personal development.

第三段（总结建议）：

In conclusion, [topic] is indispensable to everyone.  
It is high time that we took effective measures to cultivate xxx habit.  

### 二、第二段常用论点方向与示例

（一）学习与能力提升角度

通用句型：  
[topic] helps us improve our learning efficiency and develop practical abilities.  
This means that if we [form the habit of + 行为], we can make steady progress in our studies.

Self-discipline 示例：  
Self-discipline helps us form good study habits and stay focused on our goals.  
If we strictly follow our study plans every day, we can gradually improve our grades.

（二）身心健康角度

通用句型：  
[topic] contributes greatly to our physical and mental health.  
When we [具体行为], we are less likely to suffer from stress and anxiety, and can enjoy a healthier life.

Self-discipline 示例：  
Self-discipline helps us control our screen time and keep a regular schedule.  
If we go to bed early and exercise regularly, we will feel more energetic and confident.

（三）人际关系与沟通能力角度

通用句型：  
[topic] enables us to build better relationships with others.  
By learning to [具体行为], we can communicate more effectively and win others' respect and trust.

Cross-cultural communication 示例：  
Cross-cultural communication abilities enable us to understand different cultures and customs.  
This helps us develop an open and inclusive attitude towards people from different backgrounds.

（四）未来就业与职业发展角度

通用句型：  
[topic] plays an important role in our future career.  
There is no denying that employers prefer those who have [品质/能力], which can greatly increase our competitiveness in the job market.

Cross-cultural communication 示例：  
Cross-cultural communication abilities allow us to work effectively in international teams,  
which is greatly beneficial to our future development in the job market.

（五）社会意义与长远影响角度

通用句型：  
[topic] is also of great importance to society as a whole.  
When more people realize the importance of [topic], our society will become more harmonious and progressive.

Environmental protection 示例：  
Environmental awareness is of great importance to the sustainable development of our society.  
If everyone saves energy and reduces waste in daily life, we can protect our planet for future generations.
